This is all about me, Oliver the Corgi!

My life has always been an adventure. When I started out on a farm I had to watch as my brothers and sisters got chosen by families until I was all alone. Then a short human showed up in a red car and ran right to me like we were destined to explore together. It was such an exciting day and mom even got a discount on me too! The other thing I learned that day is that I hate leashes, I like to lead the group! As payback I made the car stinky the whole way home. Now I have grown up to be a very tall and proper gentleman who still has a love for adventure.
